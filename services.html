Rapid Security Posture Assessment (RSPA)

▼

The RSPA is a fast, fixed-fee assessment of your organization's real world information security posture with consideration of your business model, resources, and constraints. Through a structured interview and targeted review, you get a concise report outlining the top three to five risks most likely to impact your organization, along with practical actions to address them.

Ideal for:

  • Organizations that need to understand “where they stand” before committing to bigger security spend
  • Companies without formal information security processes who want a simple, prioritized starting point
  • Teams encountering their first signs of security pressure (e.g., customer questionnaires, insurance renewals, basic compliance asks, etc.)
  • Leadership wanting a quick, objective readout to guide budgeting or roadmap discussions
  • Businesses with small IT teams that need clarity, not ongoing oversight or long-term engagement

Price: $950

Incident & Crisis Management Package (ICMP)

▼

Information security incidents are unavoidable and planning for them can be the difference between customer trust and customer exodus. The ICMP includes a review of your operations, assets, and risks to identify the most likely and most dangerous scenarios that could harm your business and establishing a plan to respond and communicate proactively.

What's included:

  • Strategic response plan aligned to modern compliance and regulatory expectations
  • Executive and leadership communication guidance
  • Development of technical response playbooks with your SMEs
  • Incident Response Team and stakeholder training
  • Tailored tabletop exercise to test and validate the response plan
  • Summary report with findings and improvements

Ideal for:

  • Mid-market organizations (100–1,500 employees) without dedicated security leadership
  • Companies that have experienced chaotic or uncoordinated incident response
  • Growing businesses adopting new technology or entering regulated markets

Price: Starting at $12,500

Price based on environment size/complexity and number/depth of technical playbooks developed.

InfoSec Leadership Program (ISLP)

▼

For organizations needing strategic information security leadership without the commitment and cost of a full-time CISO, the ISLP provides a cost effective middle ground. Tailored to the needs of your organization, this fractional CISO engagement will rapidly establish or strengthen your security posture with a focus on fundamentals and high-value quick wins. Over time this will evolve into an integrated, scalable security program that can be leveraged as a competitive advantage.

Key Outcomes:

  • Clear security roadmap and governance framework
  • Leadership alignment on priorities and risk exposure
  • Execution of high-value initiatives that strengthen operational resilience
  • Risk visibility, reporting, and audit readiness
  • Development of security culture and internal capabilities

Ideal for:

  • Companies too small for a CISO but needing strategic security leadership
  • Private Equity firms aiming to protect and grow portfolio companies
  • Small SaaS businesses under audit pressure from customers, investors, or regulators
  • Managed Service Providers expanding into security offerings

Price: Starting at $3,500/month

Price based on scope, frequency, and organizational size/complexity.
