For Individuals & Families

▼

You don't need to be a tech expert to protect yourself online. Operation Hard Target (OHT) is our comprehensive digital self-defense workshop designed to give everyday people the skills and tools to defend against the most common cyber threats.

Operation Hard Target Workshop

A practical, hands-on training program covering:

  • Social engineering tactics and how to spot them
  • Monitoring for account compromise and how to respond
  • Establishing a credit freeze to protect against identity theft
  • Using a password manager to implement unique, strong credentials without having to remember them
  • Conducting an asset inventory and hardening your critical accounts with strong Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
  • Contingency planning and testing for account recovery for when calamity strikes

Who this is for: Individuals and families who want to take control of their digital security without becoming cybersecurity experts. If you've ever felt overwhelmed by online threats or unsure where to start, this workshop will give you clarity and confidence.

For Small Businesses

▼

Small businesses are prime targets for cyberattacks because they often lack dedicated security resources. We help you identify vulnerabilities, train your team, and implement practical security controls that fit your budget and operations.

Security Assessments

Comprehensive gap analysis against industry frameworks like NIST CSF or CIS 18, followed by prioritized recommendations for security control implementation and improvement. We identify what matters most and help you focus your resources where they'll have the greatest impact.

Team Security Training

Operation Hard Target for teams - equipping your employees with the awareness and skills to recognize threats, avoid common mistakes, and become your first line of defense.

Network Security Consulting

Network architecture review, security configuration, and ongoing support to ensure your infrastructure is protected against modern threats.

For Growing Enterprises

▼

As your organization scales, security complexity grows. You need strategic leadership but may not be ready for a full-time CISO. We provide executive-level security guidance, compliance support, and program development to mature your security posture.

Virtual CISO (vCISO) Services

Part-time or fractional CISO services for organizations that need strategic security leadership without the cost of a full-time executive. We help you build and manage security programs, communicate risk to stakeholders, and make informed decisions about security investments.

Ideal for: Organizations without dedicated security teams who need experienced leadership to guide their security strategy.

Compliance Support

Implementation and ongoing support for compliance frameworks including:

  • SOC 2 readiness
  • CMMC readiness
  • ISO 27001 implementation
  • PCI-DSS requirements
  • HIPAA compliance

Security Program Development

Building security programs from the ground up - policies, procedures, incident response plans, and security awareness programs tailored to your organization's risk profile and operational needs.
